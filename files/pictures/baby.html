Oya Baka

"Oya Baka" is a Japanese term that can be charitably translated as "foolish parents." It's basically the new parent syndrome, where the new parents overwhelm relatives, friends, the neighbors, the neighbor's dog, etc. etc. with pictures and stories of the new baby. "See little xxxx with his new teeth! yada yada yada." Rather then making you suffer through this in person, we decided to do it electronically. So, here's the first pictures of our new baby!
