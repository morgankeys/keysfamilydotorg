Challenging Sushi

The Anti J-Pop

Welcome to Challenging Sushi!  Challenging Sushi is an audio stream focused on (at least what I think) are interesting and innovative Japanese musicians.  Much of the music here is relatively obscure and genres represented are rock, reggae, dub, punk, folk rock, new wave, electronica, folk, and some things that just defy classification.  If you're interested in the latest Japanese hits from really cute girls or boys or rock from people who spend more time on their makeup then their guitar licks, then there are many other audio streams that you can listen to, and we'll both be happier.  However, if you're interested in hearing Japanese musicians playing quality music in a variety of styles and quite often pushing the boundaries, then take a listen!

Fair Warning:  Some of the songs broadcast on the stream contain adult language, in both English and Japanese (can you believe?)
