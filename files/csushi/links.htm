Other Internet Radio Stations

  • My other radio station http://launch.yahoo.com/lc/?rt=0&rp1=0&rp2=1245079799  This is a station that I "influence" as part of my broadband service.  Basically, I rate the artists they have available and the station uses that info to come up with the radio stream. I think I've got it whipped into relatively good shape.
  • Radio LavaLamp:  http://www.radiolavalamp.org A community Internet radio station run out of Osaka.  Great mixture of underreported political information and alternative music from around the world.
  • Muneo House:  http://www.live365.com/stations/221312  Unfortunately, you can only hear this if you're a preferred member at Live365, which is too bad since this is a great collection of relatively rare Japanese political comedy.  Suzuki Muneo is a disgraced former member of the Japanese parliament who was brought down by corruption surrounding a project of his called "Muneo House" among other things.  The artist then took his speeches and set them to house electronica.  At least the web site is still up and definitely worth a look   http://www.koalanet.ne.jp/~twotrue/munex/ 
  • Anago Radio:  http://www.live365.com/stations/298569  Again, another station that is only available to Live365 preferred listeners, but worthwhile if you've spent some time in the Kansai area.
  • Radio Fighto:  http://www.live365.com/stations/309123  A station specializing in Japanese and other Asian punk, power and goofy pop.

Online Music Shops

  • Far Side Music:  http://www.farsidemusic.com/  Heard something on Challenging Sushi and would like to buy the CD?  Well, if it's still being manufactured, chances are this is the place that you'll be able to find it.  Run by Paul Fisher, the gentleman behind the "Rough Guide to the Music of Japan" CD, this site specializes in lesser known albums from Japan and other parts of Asia.
